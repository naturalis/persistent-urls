Naturalis PURL server up and running!

Welcome to the Naturalis PURL server. Naturalis is in the process of creating persistent URLs (PURLs) for its main assets. Currently we have PURLs for our collection objects (specimens). You are requested to refer to our specimens by means of their PURL. For more information, see our documentation on github.

Specimen PURLs

PURLs for our collection objects take the following form:

@BASEURL@naturalis/specimen/{ObjectID}

Substitute {ObjectID} with an actual specimen ID. For example: @BASEURL@naturalis/specimen/U.1040749
