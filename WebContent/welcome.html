Naturalis PURL server

Welcome to the Naturalis PURL server. Naturalis is in the process of creating persistent URLs (PURLs) for its main assets. Currently we have PURLs for our collection objects (specimens) and our multimedia objects (mostly images of collection objects). You are requested to refer to our specimens and multimedia by means of their PURL, rather than through URLs from, for example, the Naturalis Bioportal or our media library. The PURL server will forward

Specimen PURLs

PURLs for our collection object take the following form:

http://localhost:8080/purl/naturalis/specimen/{ObjectID}

Substitute {ObjectID} with an actual specimen ID. For example: http://localhost:8080/purl/naturalis/specimen/U.1040749

Multimedia PURLs

PURLs for our collection object take the following form:

http://localhost:8080/purl/naturalis/multimedia/{ObjectID}

Substitute {ObjectID} with an actual specimen ID. For example: http://localhost:8080/purl/naturalis/multimedia/U.1040749:383445478
